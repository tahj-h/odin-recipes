Orange chicken Recipe

  • 1 cup orange marmalade
  • 1/2 cup Kansas City-style BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup low sodium soy sauce
  • 1 (1 pound) bag frozen fully cooked chicken nuggets
  • Sliced green onions
  • sesame seeds
  1. Step 1.Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Place frozen nuggets in a single layer on a baking sheet.
  2. Step 2. Bake in the preheated oven until hot and crispy, 11 to 13 minutes, or according to package directions.
  3. Step 3. Meanwhile, whisk marmalade, BBQ sauce, and soy sauce together in a small saucepan and heat over low heat until hot, about 5 minutes.
  4. Step 4. Place nuggets in a large bowl. Drizzle sauce over the top. Toss to coat.
